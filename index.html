About me

Hi there! I'm Mel - passionate dance teacher and choreographer based in the Shreveport/Bossier area, specializing in jazz and hip-hop. I began dancing at a young age, and it has been the driving force behind my creativity and career ever since. Throughout my journey, I’ve trained with nationally recognized programs and respected collegiate dance teams. These experiences have shaped my technical foundation, performance quality, and leadership style as an instructor. Dance is more than movement to me — it’s about building confidence, discipline, and artistry in every performer I teach.
